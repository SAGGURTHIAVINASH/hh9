About Women's Safety

Women's safety is a critical issue that affects women worldwide. We aim to educate, empower, and provide the necessary tools to help ensure the safety of women everywhere.

Safety Tips

  • Always be aware of your surroundings.
  • Avoid walking alone in dimly lit areas.
  • Keep your phone fully charged and accessible.
  • Trust your instincts; if something feels off, remove yourself from the situation.
  • Share your whereabouts with a trusted friend or family member.

Emergency Resources

If you are in immediate danger, contact your local authorities:

  • Police Emergency Number: 911 (USA)
  • National Domestic Violence Hotline: 1-800-799-SAFE
  • Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network (RAINN): 1-800-656-HOPE
  • National Coalition Against Domestic Violence
  • RAINN - Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network
  • Women's Health.gov
